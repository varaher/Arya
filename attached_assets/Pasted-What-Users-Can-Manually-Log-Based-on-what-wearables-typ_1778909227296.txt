What Users Can Manually Log
Based on what wearables typically track — here's what makes sense for ARYA:
Daily vitals:

Steps
Sleep duration + quality (deep / light / restless)
Resting heart rate
Blood oxygen (SpO2)
Stress level (many devices give a 1–100 score)
Calories burned

Weekly:

Weight
Body fat % (if they have a smart scale)
Blood pressure (if they have a BP monitor)

For diabetic users:

Blood glucose readings
HbA1c (quarterly)

For women:

Cycle tracking
Basal body temperature


How ARYA Uses This Data
This is where it gets powerful. ARYA doesn't just store numbers — it connects them to everything else it knows.
Examples of what ARYA says:

"Your resting heart rate has been elevated for 4 days — 78 vs your usual 64. Your mood logs show stress this week. These two are connected. What's weighing on you?"


"You slept 4.5 hours last night and have your Sequoia call today. Want to push the hardest decisions to tomorrow when you're clearer?"


"Your steps dropped from 8,000 to 2,000 over the last week. The last time this happened, your mood dropped three days later. Just flagging it."


"Your SpO2 has been below 95 three times this week. That's worth mentioning to a doctor — not urgent, but worth noting."

This is something no fitness app does — because no fitness app knows your mood, your goals, your decisions, and your calendar simultaneously.

The Three Phases
Phase 1 — Manual entry (build now)
Simple daily log. Quick entry. 30 seconds max.
Phase 2 — Health app sync

Apple Health (iOS) — free API, pulls from any connected device
Google Fit (Android) — free API, same
This covers 90% of users without any direct device partnership

Phase 3 — Direct device integration

boAt, Noise — India's biggest wearable brands
Fitbit, Garmin, Samsung Health
These require partnership agreements but unlock automatic sync


The UI I'd Build
Three screens:
1. Daily Health Log — quick entry card in the morning briefing. Six metrics, tap to update. Takes 30 seconds.
2. Health Trends — weekly view. ARYA's interpretation of what the numbers mean together — not just charts.
3. ARYA Health Insights — where the magic lives. ARYA connecting health data to mood, decisions, and goals. "Your best decision-making days correlate with 7+ hours of sleep and under 70bpm resting heart rate."

One Important Caution
Health data falls under sensitive personal data in India's DPDP Act 2023 — stricter requirements than regular personal data.
You need:

Explicit consent before collecting any health data
Clear purpose limitation — health data used only for ARYA insights, never shared
Right to delete health data separately from other data
Consider adding it to your Right to Forget flow as a separate category
