## The Biggest Gap Overall

Looking at all 7 screenshots together — **the translation is 70% complete.** The structure works, Malayalam renders correctly, major UI strings are translated. What's missing is the **granular strings** — emoji labels, menu items, suggestion cards, button labels inside modals.

These are all `t()` wiring issues — the translations probably exist in your 38-key file but the components are still using hardcoded strings.

Search the codebase for:
```
"Rough" → replace with t('mood.rough')
"My Goals" → replace with t('nav.goals')  
"Sign Out" → replace with t('auth.signout')
"Customize ARYA" → replace with t('nav.customize')
```

That one search-and-replace pass will fix most of these at once.
