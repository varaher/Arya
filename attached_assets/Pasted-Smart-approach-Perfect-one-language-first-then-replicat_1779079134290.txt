## The Hindi Standard — What We're Building

**The principle:** When Hindi is selected, every single thing the user sees and hears should feel like ARYA was born speaking Hindi — not translated from English.

```
Wrong (translated feel):
  "आपके लक्ष्य" — correct grammar, feels like Google Translate
  "ARYA के साथ बात करें" — stiff, textbook

Right (native friend feel):
  "तुम्हारे goals" — how friends actually talk
  "ARYA से बात करो" — natural, warm
```
