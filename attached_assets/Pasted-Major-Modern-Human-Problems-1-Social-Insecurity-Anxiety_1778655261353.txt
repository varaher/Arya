Major Modern Human Problems
1. Social Insecurity & Anxiety
People constantly feel:
“Am I enough?”
“Do I fit in?”
“What will others think?”
Why it’s increasing:
Social media comparison culture
Fear of missing out (FOMO)
Pressure to appear successful
Reduced real human connection
Career uncertainty
Relationship instability
Result:
Anxiety disorders
Burnout
Loneliness
Emotional exhaustion
Validation addiction
2. Lack of Self-Worth
Many people now tie their value to:
Salary
Followers
Appearance
Brand ownership
Relationship status
External approval
Earlier, identity came from:
Family role
Community
Skills
Values
Character
Now identity often comes from:
Online image
Social status
Public perception
Result:
Low confidence
Depression
Fear of failure
Constant comparison
Need for attention and validation
3. Income Inequality & Material Showoff
This is becoming psychologically dangerous.
People today are exposed daily to:
Luxury lifestyles
Expensive gadgets
Exotic vacations
“Perfect” families
Influencer culture
Even middle-class individuals now compare themselves with millionaires online.
Effects:
Dissatisfaction despite stable life
Debt culture
Stress to “look rich”
Fake lifestyle maintenance
Relationship strain
Jealousy and resentment
This creates:
“Visible wealth pressure.”
Not everyone wants luxury — but many feel inadequate without it.
