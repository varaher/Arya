### Voice metering — the numbers that matter

Sarvam AI STT pricing is approximately ₹1–2 per minute of audio.

```
50 free minutes costs you:    ₹75–100/month (~$1.20)
Acceptable within Pro margin

User goes to 200 minutes:
Your cost:  ₹200–400
You charge: 150 mins × $0.05 = $7.50 extra
You net:    ~$5.50 on overages alone
```

This is how Twilio, AWS, and every API company makes their real money — the base plan is the door, the overages are the business.
